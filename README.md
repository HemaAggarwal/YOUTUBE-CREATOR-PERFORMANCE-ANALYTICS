📊 Key Insights
- Analyzed YouTube channel performance by tracking key metrics such as views, watch time, subscribers gained, engagement rate, and revenue, providing a complete overview of channel growth.
- Identified the top-performing videos based on views, watch time, and audience engagement, helping determine which content generated the highest impact.
- Compared channel performance across different months and years to identify growth trends, seasonal patterns, and fluctuations in audience activity.
- Analyzed audience engagement through metrics such as likes, comments, shares, and average view duration, helping evaluate content effectiveness.
- Examined traffic sources and audience behavior to understand how viewers discovered the channel and which sources contributed the most views and engagement.
- Built interactive visualizations and filters to allow users to analyze performance by video category, upload date, content type, and other key metrics, making the dashboard flexible and easy to explore.
- Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables content creators to monitor channel performance, identify successful content strategies, and make data-driven decisions to improve audience growth and engagement.
-Tools Used: Power BI | Excel | DAX | Power Query.
