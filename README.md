📊 Key Insights
- Analyzed YouTube creator performance using 847M total views, 5M subscribers, $3.51M revenue, 9.21% average engagement rate, and 8.3% average CTR, providing a complete overview of channel growth and audience interaction.
- Compared monthly performance across 2024 and 2025, identifying fluctuations in views and subscriber growth to understand seasonal trends and content performance over time.
- Identified the top 5 performing videos, with the highest-performing video crossing 10M views, helping determine which content generated the strongest audience engagement.
- Analyzed content category performance, where Fitness and Technology generated the highest revenue, helping identify the most profitable content niches.
- Evaluated traffic source distribution and found that Suggested Videos contributed the highest share of traffic, highlighting the importance of YouTube's recommendation system in driving views.
- Compared views against engagement rate to understand how audience interaction varied across different levels of video performance and identify content with strong viewer retention.
Measured CTR across different content categories, helping evaluate which types of videos attracted the highest click-through rates and audience interest.
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with dynamic filters for Year, Country, and Category, enabling users to explore creator performance, audience behavior, revenue trends, and content effectiveness for data-driven decision-making.
Tools Used: Power BI | Excel | DAX | Power Query.
